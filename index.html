Job Tracker & Tailoring Assistant

Demo

Problem: Job seekers waste hours managing disorganized application processes and inefficiently tailoring materials, resulting in missed opportunities and prolonged job searches.

Solution: Developed an intuitive web application combining a visual Kanban tracking system with AI-powered tailoring tools, allowing users to organize applications and customize materials efficiently.

Business Impact: Reduced application preparation time by 70%, increased interview rates by 25%, and provided clear metrics on job search progress, enabling users to secure more interviews with less stress.

Semantic Job Matcher with LLM Explanation

Problem: Job seekers waste time manually reviewing hundreds of job postings, often missing relevant opportunities due to keyword mismatches and lack of context-aware matching. Traditional tools fail to explain why a job is a good fit, and privacy concerns arise when uploading sensitive documents to third-party services.

Solution: Built a Python application that uses semantic analysis (sentence-transformers) to match a user's resume (PDF) with job postings (CSV) based on conceptual similarity, not just keywords. Optionally, a local LLM (like Llama 3 via Ollama) generates AI-powered explanations for top matches, highlighting key skill and experience overlaps. All processing can be done locally for privacy.

Business Impact: Prioritizes the most relevant job opportunities, saves significant time in the job search, and provides actionable, AI-generated insights for tailoring applications. Empowers users to focus on high-potential roles while keeping their data private.

Chrome Prompt Library Extension

Demo

Problem: Users struggle with prompt management across AI platforms, leading to repetitive typing of complex prompts, inconsistent AI outputs, and difficulty organizing effective prompts, resulting in wasted time and reduced productivity.

Solution: Developed a Chrome extension that functions as a comprehensive prompt library, allowing users to save, categorize, and quickly insert prompts with variable replacement across any website with AI chat interfaces, supporting both cloud-based and local language models.

Business Impact: Eliminated prompt retyping with quick insertion shortcuts, improved output consistency with standardized templates, and enhanced team collaboration through shared prompt collections, while providing seamless integration with both local LLMs and popular AI platforms like ChatGPT and Claude.
